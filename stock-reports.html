üìò Key Stock Market Terms

Bull Market

A market in which prices are rising or are expected to rise, usually by 20% or more.

Bear Market

A market condition where stock prices fall 20% or more from recent highs, often due to investor pessimism.

Blue Chip Stocks

Shares of large, reputable companies known for stability, profitability, and long-term performance.

IPO (Initial Public Offering)

The process by which a private company becomes publicly traded on a stock exchange.
